Cardio

At intensify, we like workouts to encompass using different energy networks for a maximum burn. Our specially structured cardio workouts will use plyometric exercises, timing and speed to blast both your aerobic and anaerobic systems, resulting in fat burning, muscle building and metabolism boosting.

Δ

Hybrid

Get the best workouts with our hybrid sessions! Combining our favorite cardio, resistance, agility and core movements, for an epic burn that increases your endurance, fires up your metabolism and improves your strength. In these sessions you’ll leave everything out on the floor to challenge both your body and your mind.

Δ

Resistance

Resistance training is an essential for building muscle mass and bone density. Our strength training techniques and use of practical movement patterns to help our clients to increase their muscle mass and burn more calories every day, even while at sleep. Each workout is designed to target the fat cells, and we help to get a solid base of strength = Resulting in stronger, leaner, more defined muscles.

Recovery

Progression and ﬂexibility are important components of physical ﬁtness. We believe that our everyday athletes need longevity in their training. Our Flexibility Recovery Sessions are designed to supplement your training sessions through a mixture of passive and active exercises and stretching techniques designed to improve ﬂexibility and posture. These are low-intensity, slow and controlled trainings with a special attention on postural awareness and body alignment.
